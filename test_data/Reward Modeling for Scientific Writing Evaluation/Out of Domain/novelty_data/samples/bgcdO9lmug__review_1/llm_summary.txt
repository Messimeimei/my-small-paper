**Summary for Reviewer:**

RePrompt is positioned within the iterative, feedback-driven prompt optimization literature, closely related to works like PromptAgent, GPO, and Self-Refine, which also leverage LLM self-reflection and intermediate feedback. The main technical novelty claimed is the use of summarization-based analysis of dialogue history to guide prompt updates, especially in agentic, multi-turn tasks where final solution checkers are unavailable. While this summarization mechanism is a specific implementation detail, the overall framework and reliance on intermediate feedback are not unique and are shared with several recent methods. The authors somewhat overstate their contribution by downplaying the overlap with prior work, particularly regarding the use of reflection and intermediate feedback. Reviewers should assess whether the summarization-based approach offers a substantive improvement over existing critique and self-reflection mechanisms, as the advance appears incremental rather than conceptual.
