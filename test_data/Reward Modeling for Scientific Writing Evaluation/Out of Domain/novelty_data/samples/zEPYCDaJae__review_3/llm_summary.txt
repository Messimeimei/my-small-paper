**Summary for Reviewer:**

This assessment finds that DataSEA’s main contribution is the integration of existing LLM-driven automation techniques—dataset search, retrieval, organization, and script generation—into a single, end-to-end system. While this unified pipeline is a substantive engineering advance, each individual component has been addressed in prior works such as Auctus (dataset search/profiling) and CAAFE (feature engineering/code generation). The novelty claims are strongest at the system integration level, but are less compelling when considering the underlying methods, which are not fundamentally new. The authors tend to understate the integrative capabilities of prior systems and omit direct comparisons to highly relevant related work, potentially overstating DataSEA’s uniqueness. Reviewers should recognize that the field is rapidly evolving with many incremental, integration-focused advances, and calibrate their evaluation of novelty and contribution accordingly.
