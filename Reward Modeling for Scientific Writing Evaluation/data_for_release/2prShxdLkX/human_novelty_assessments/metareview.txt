This paper presents MoDGS, a dynamic scene modeling algorithm using casually captured monocular videos that integrates single-view depth estimation, 3D-aware initialization, and robust ordinal depth loss to overcome the rapid camera motion assumption in previous methods. The proposed components are technically novel and interesting, with the 3D-aware initialization improving the consistency of the initial Gaussians and the ordinal depth loss addressing the problem of depth consistency across frames. While the method builds incrementally on existing approaches such as deformable 3DGS by optimizing depth consistency and deformation, its performance depends significantly on external single-view depth estimators, which may limit the algorithm in challenging conditions. All reviewers agree that the technical contribution of the proposed algorithm is significant.
