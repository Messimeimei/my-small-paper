The paper proposes Physics-Informed Autoencoders (PIAEs) to address gaps in carbon dioxide (CO2) emission measurements, specifically for Net Ecosystem Exchange (NEE) data from agricultural fields. According to the reviews, the paper has weaknesses including limited novelty, as the work mainly applies existing methods to a specific domain. There is a lack of broader applicability and justification for key design elements, such as the two-phase training process. Summing up, while the results are promising, the paper's complexity and missing details limit its impact on a general machine-learning audience.
