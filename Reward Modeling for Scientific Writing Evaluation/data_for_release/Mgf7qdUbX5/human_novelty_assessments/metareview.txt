The paper presents interesting ideas and makes some progress, but the limited scope of experimentation on only the PKU Beavertails dataset restricts the generalizability and scientific impact of the results, making it difficult to assess the robustness and practical utility of the proposed defense method. While the additional experiments on perplexity-based and round-trip based defense significantly improve the paper, the lack of integration of the RAP defense method leaves a gap in the comprehensiveness of the study and weakens the contribution. These unresolved issues, particularly the limited experimental validation, diminish the paper's suitability for publication at this time.
