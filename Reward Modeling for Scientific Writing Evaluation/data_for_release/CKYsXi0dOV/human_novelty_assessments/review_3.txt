This paper presents BLIP-3-Video, which introduces a "temporal encoder" alongside a conventional visual tokenizer, allowing it to significantly reduce visual tokens (32 tokens compared to thousands in other models). However, the primary weakness is the insufficient novelty. As detailed in Section 2.2, the only improvements to TTM include (1) time-stamped positional encodings and (2) a 'grouped' TTM temporal encoder. These minor changes do not substantiate a significant contribution.
