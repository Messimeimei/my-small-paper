This paper introduces SMI-TED, an encoder-decoder Transformer-based model for learning representations of molecules that can be used for downstream tasks such as reaction yield prediction. The approach seems similar to MolFormer (Ross et al., 2022): pre-training of a Transformer model using a BERT-like approach for downstream adaptation for various molecular regression/classification tasks. The main differences seem to be the additional decoder (and reconstruction loss) and the ensembling of different representations; however, the ensembling does not actually seem to be used for many of the tasks.
