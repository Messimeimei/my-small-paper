This paper proposes a weaker sufficient condition for high dimensional bandit algorithm and proves regret bounds for bandit algorithms under this weaker condition. I think in general the weaker condition, and the algorithm induced, would be a good addition to the literature. The novelty claimed, such as the cyclic induction (Lemma 6?), should be moved to the main text as well. Assumption 3 is claimed to be "strictly weaker" than the existing conditions, but some counterexamples should be provided to show that Assumption 3 holds but not any of the existing conditions. It's hard to read and understand the novelty for the current version.
