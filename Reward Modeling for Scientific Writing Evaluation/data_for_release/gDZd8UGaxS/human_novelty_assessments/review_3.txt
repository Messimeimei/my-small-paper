This paper presents a method for incremental learning that comprises two key components: (1) multiple experts and (2) prototype compensation. Though significant performance improvements are demonstrated, ProCEED shows limited novelty as it simply combines the approaches of Rypeść et al. (2023) (multiple experts) with Zhou et al. (2024b) (prototype compensation), and there is no fundamental innovation in the respective components compared to previous work. In addition, there are some same sentences as in previous literature, such as the description of the model architecture and the formulation of the prototype realignment problem, which closely mirror those in Rypeść et al. (2023) and Zhou et al. (2024b).
