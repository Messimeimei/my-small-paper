This paper presents an LMM-based NR-PCQA method that is capable of consuming text, images and point clouds to predict quality scores. Both novelty and contribution of the paper are limited. The introduced model, database construction, and model design are all based on the existing LMM-based IQA and VQA studies. Only small adaptions are made to adapt the format of 3D point clouds, which heavily limits the novelty and contribution of the paper.
