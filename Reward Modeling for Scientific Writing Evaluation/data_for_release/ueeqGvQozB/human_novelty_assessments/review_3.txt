This paper focuses on developing a benchmark ML4MILP to evaluate machine learning (ML) based methods for solving mixed-integer linear programs of MILPs. I have questions about how this benchmark compares and contrasts against the benchmark created for the ML4CO Neurips competition (Gasse et al., 2022), and how this competition benchmark (and the existence of MIPlib) affect the "first open-source benchmark dataset" claim in line 74.
