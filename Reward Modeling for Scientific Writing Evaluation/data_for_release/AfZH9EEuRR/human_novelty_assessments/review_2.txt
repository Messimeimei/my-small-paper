This work introduces a system for reading QR codes from first-person perspectives on wearable devices, tackling challenges like wide field-of-view and code distortion using a detection and decoding approach that includes a tailored Faster R-CNN model and image enhancement techniques. However, I find a lack of novelty, as the paper builds upon well-established techniques such as Faster R-CNN for detection and common image enhancement methods for decoding. While the system demonstrates a significant 34% improvement in QR code reading success over existing state-of-the-art readers, I would appreciate explanations on the novelty of this system to better understand its unique contributions compared to prior work.
