The authors propose an approach LP-FT to tackle the key challenge of balancing local personalization and global generalization by combining linear probing with full fine-tuning and providing a solid theoretical analysis using a two-layer linear network. The novelty of the proposed method is somehow limited, as it is mostly based on LP-FT. While the contribution on comprehensive analysis is significant, the novelty of the technical method seems weak. The overall approach of using linear probing with full fine-tuning to prevent feature distortion is interesting, but the paper would benefit from more comparison with PFL baselines, as it only considers different fine-tuning paradigms.
