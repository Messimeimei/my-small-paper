This paper introduces Diffusion Policy Policy Optimization (DPPO), an algorithmic framework that fine-tunes diffusion-based policies using policy gradient optimization (PPO) from reinforcement learning, and presents a novel dual-layer MDP framework along with several best practices such as fine-tuning only the last few denoising steps and using DDIM sampling to improve efficiency and performance. However, compared to QSM, DPPO does not introduce particularly novel concepts but rather provides engineering or empirical adjustments, such as fine-tuning certain denoising steps and replacing value estimation with advantage estimation.
