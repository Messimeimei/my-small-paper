This paper addresses the task of dynamic scene reconstruction, specifically developing a deformable 3DGS representation from an unposed monocular video. However, there is a lack of novelty, as the proposed method is more like a mixed bag which combines [1], [2], [3], and [4]. Specifically, the hexplane-based deformable Gaussian field has been explored by [1], the relative pose initialization is adopted from [2], the reprojection loss in Eq. 12 is similar to that of [3], and the depth alignment loss in Eq. 13 is similar to Eq. 15 in [4]. The authors may have to make their contributions more explicit.
