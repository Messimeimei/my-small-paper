This paper proposes a novel Transformer-based forecasting model tailored to observability, with key contributions including a novel transformer block integrating one channel-mixing attention layer followed by multiple blocks of time-wise attention, and an output head employing a mixture of Student-t distributions. The design of proportional factorized space-time attention potentially offers an expressive and efficient backbone for multivariate time series modeling. However, the technical contributions of space-time attention and Student-t mixture heads are of limited novelty and provide marginal improvement. If domain-specific training data is a contribution, details are needed on the pre-training corpus construction, ablation studies, and novelty of data selection.
