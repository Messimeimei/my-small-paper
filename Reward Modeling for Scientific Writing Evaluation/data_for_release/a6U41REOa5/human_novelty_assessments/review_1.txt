This paper introduces a retrieval-augmented diffusion framework for antibody design and optimization, with its key contribution being the retrieval of structurally-similar protein segments to the CDR for use as conditions in diffusion generation. Although the paper is the first to introduce retrieval-augmented diffusion in antibody design, there has been retrieval-augmented diffusion model proposed in other applications, such as in [1] and [2] (protein-specific 3D molecule generation), so the method novelty is fair due to the prior works. The paper needs to also cite previous retrieval-augmented generative models, and clearly state the contribution in terms of the method.
